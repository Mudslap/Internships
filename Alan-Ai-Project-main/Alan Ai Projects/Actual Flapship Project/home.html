Our Mission

Gaming is one of the most entertaining things for both kids and adults. However millions of disabled people, such as amputees and people with medical problems, aren't able to play these enjoyable pixels on our screen just like us. By incorporating Alan AI into custom built games, people are able to play enjoyable games anywhere no matter what medical issues they have.

Voice Games is an internship project built by a middle school student for Alan AI and PilotCity.
